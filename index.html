NEW YORK SURROGACY CENTER

New York Surrogacy Center is a licensed surrogacy agency that matches surrogates and prospective parents and guides them through the surrogacy journey. Our uniquely focused agency prides itself on competent and compassionate individual attention to the surrogates and intended parents from the first contact with us through delivery of the baby.

New York Surrogacy Center was licensed by the New York State Department of Health to match New York surrogates on May 21, 2021 (License Number GS211000).

Our experience with NYSC has been fantastic. Casey, Joe, and the entire team are kind, responsive, and competent. We were quickly matched with a wonderful surrogate, and weve been able to take advantage of NYSCs top notch industry connections with attorneys and fertility clinics. We have been so pleased with NYSCs personalized approach, and they have made our surrogacy as seamless as possible. We are so thankful to have found NYSC!
